Firebrand Assembly of God is a church that exists to be a source of hope for the hopeless

Following the word of life and the footprints of Jesus, we aim to be a source of salvation for people by spreading the word of God, developing of the community we live in, expanding of the church of God, feeding the hungry and healing the grieved.
